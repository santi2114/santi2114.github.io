VILLARREAL CONQUERS THE EUROPA LEAGUE

Villarreal lifted the UEFA Europa League after beating Manchester United (1-1) in a historic final that was held on May 26, 2021 in Gdansk.

A hard and precious road to the FINAL

In the round of 32, the 'groguet' team was drawn against UEFA Champions League rival FC Salzburg. Villarreal beat the Austrian team in both games (0-2 and 2-1) and qualified for the round of 16.


The next rival was Dynamo kyiv, which the yellows decisively defeated. A 0-2 and a 2-0 served for the 'groguets' to get the ticket for the quarterfinals.


Dinamo Zagreb was the next hurdle for Unai Emery's team. The yellows got a valuable booty from their visit to the Croatian capital. They won 0-1 and sealed their place in the semifinals with another victory at the Estadio de la Cerámica (2-1).

Groguet fervor in Vila-real

Thousands of Submarino fans poured into the streets to celebrate the UEFA Europa League title a day after the historic triumph in Gdansk.


The yellow club organized a parade of champions to offer the trophy to their fans. Under the motto 'Europa és nostra', the team made a complete tour of the city of Vila-real with an open-top bus. Starting from the Estadio de la Cerámica, it passed through the main streets and avenues of the city before returning again to the groguet fiefdom.
