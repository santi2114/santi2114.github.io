CHAMPIONS LEAGUE

After having reached the top of the Europa League and winning it, Villarreal earned the right to dream in the fourth adventure for the 2021-2022 Champions League, the most important club competition on the planet.

GROUP STAGE

Villarreal have a difficult group in this group stage: Manchester United, Atalanta and Young Boys.


Against those teams he managed to win both games against Young Boys (1-4 and 2-0), he lost both games against Manchester United (2-1 and 2-0) and against Atalanta they tied the first game at home (2-2) and won the second leg (2-3).


Finally they went to the Round of 16 of the Champions League, the yellow team's rival in the round of 16 was Juventus of Turin.
